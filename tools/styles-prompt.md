# Styles graphiques

## Givre Épique" (ou "Frost-Bound Epicism" / "Lumina-Gothic Digital" en anglais)

```
Style: High-fantasy digital oil painting, dramatic chiaroscuro with intense rim lighting, ethereal atmospheric perspective. Technique: Sharp focus on central textures with impressionistic, blurred backgrounds. Lighting: Blinding cinematic glow, high contrast, bloom effects, and lens flare. Textures: Polished metallic reflections, weathered surfaces, and fluid motion blur. Aesthetics: Gritty yet majestic, chaotic particle effects (snow, dust, sparks), vibrant accent colors against a desaturated, cool-toned palette. Inspired by epic concept art for TCG and high-end fantasy illustrations.
```
